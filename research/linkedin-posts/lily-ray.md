I just noticed his site is continuing to surge in organic \& AI search visibility, with a huge increase in AI Overview citations since the May core update.



To me, the site/brand is the ultimate embodiment of E-E-A-T:

\- Experience: Rick has spent \~4 months a year in Europe for decades, walking the routes and updating guidebooks annually

\## Post 3 — Comparison/Alternative Pages as the Next Spam Target (1 week ago)

https://www.linkedin.com/posts/lily-ray-44755615\_i-think-the-next-frontier-of-spam-fighting-activity-7472261355274817538-Xv\_f/



I think the next frontier of spam fighting from search/AI companies is going to hit comparison and alternative pages (Brand X vs. Y, Competitor Alternatives). "Build a lot of these" is advice I keep hearing at conferences and in industry blogs.



The fundamental problem: Google's product review criteria require proving you actually tried and tested the product. For a brand comparing itself to competitors, meeting that bar means hiring your competitor's service and authentically reviewing it — almost no brand does this. Instead, brands lean on "research" or negative reviews, sometimes inventing information to make themselves look better.



I believe Google, OpenAI, and other AI companies will increasingly lean on Reddit, YouTube reviews, Trustpilot, and G2 instead of biased brand content. Building comparison pages at scale and doing it inauthentically is risky — already seeing sites hit by Google's late January update for this pattern.

\## Post 4 — Google Sheets AI Formula for Keyword-to-Prompt Conversion (2 weeks ago)

https://www.linkedin.com/posts/lily-ray-44755615\_heres-a-little-hack-ive-been-using-to-quickly-activity-7470166067043147776-6kTD



A hack for converting SEO keywords into relevant AI prompts at scale directly in Google Sheets, using the =AI or =Gemini function with a custom prompt. This allows taking keywords with real search volume (from Ahrefs, Semrush, Similarweb, etc.) and converting them into the natural-language queries people would actually type or speak to an AI assistant, preserving search intent type (informational, commercial, transactional, local).



Prompt used: "You are converting a terse SEO search keyword into the natural-language query a real person would actually type or speak to an AI assistant like ChatGPT, Gemini, or a voice assistant. Rewrite the keyword as a single conversational question or request that preserves the exact search intent and intent type... Return only the rewritten prompt as plain text — no quotation marks, no preamble, no explanation."
